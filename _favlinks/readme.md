## Description

FavLinks is a bookmarks site that allows you to categorize links to 
sites that you love to browse frequently. Some may be thinking. 
That's why I have by browser synced. That may be true. But, now you 
don't need to be locked into the hassle of syncing your bookmarks 
across your devices. You just go to the URL and there it is. All of 
your favorite links for viewing.
