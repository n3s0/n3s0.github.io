## Summary
---

I am not formally active within the lock picking community, lock smithing
community, or the lock sport community. But, I have found that I need to step up
my game a little bit. We must start somewhere. So, I will start from the very
beginning of the [Lock Pickers United Belt Explorer](https://lpubelts.com/#/locks)
ranking system. Providing submissions using this site.

The submission requirements for the `White belt` teir. Are pretty
self-explainatory. Submission requirements require picking any lock with any
tool. The evidence format being either photo or video with the turned core
visable.

I will use this page to link notes to locks I've researched, picked, and gutted
that fall within the `White belt` tier. Think of it as a splash page for the
[locks](https://lpubelts.com/#/locks?tab=White) listed within this tier. The
other locks I post may fall under other categories. But, if interested. The
search is available on the site to search different locks. In the future there
will be a dedicated page for different locks containing my experiences within
the catalog/library.

I would recomend those who are looking to take up this challenge to review the
[submission requirements](https://www.reddit.com/r/lockpicking/wiki/beltranking/#wiki_belt_request_instructions) 
for the different platforms along with the guidelines for 
[video submissions](https://www.reddit.com/r/lockpicking/wiki/beltranking/#wiki_video_requirements) 
provided by Lock Pickers United. Although this is here. This is merely something 
for me to look back on and those making the descision to grant ranking may not 
want to go through a large article that is linked to other articles for your 
submission. I've observed that long form discussion or evidence is required 
when working towards your Orange or Green belt.

## White Belt Requirements
---

Here are the belt requirements for the `White belt` ranking from the Lock
Pickers United Belt Explorer.

> This is the beginning of your journey of self-discovery that will test the 
> limits of your mind, body, and spirit. Your belt is white because you are 
> like freshly fallen snow, pure, true of heart and ready to be molded into a 
> lockpicking machine.
> 
> Both photos and videos of your picking accomplishment are accepted for this 
> rank.
> 
> **Requirements**
> 
> - Pick any lock with any tool.
> 
> **Evidence format**
> 
> - Photos or vídeos, showing turned core.
