explanation of the standards associated around how I build the skeleton
and build documentation for my projects. This is mostly for my own
reference. Mostly because the best way to generate documentation is
to first know how your documentation should be structured.

On top of that, I will document how software is developed. This will
take a few revisions I'm sure. Better ways can come to light in the
future. Just a matter of when or whether it's really worth exploring.
